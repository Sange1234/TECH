Tech-Start-up strives to make the investment space more inclusive by granting retail investors access to opportunities that were previously available only to a very small portion of the population.Lower minimums provides a broader section of the population an opportunity to participate. At the same time,we want to make it easier for founders to raise and create their own community of engaged users.

This platform (Tech-Start-up) is one of the best ways to invest in startups. With a minimum ticket size of $49, one can become an angel investor and invest in start-ups they believe in, to grow their wealth exponentially. Traditional angel investing was an offline process, reserved for only HNIs. This inspired the formation of Tech-Start-up, to enable private investments online and reduce the minimum ticket size to make it accessible to all. For start-ups, this is the only way to raise from their community (personal and professional network) while maintaining a clean cap table.

If you are sick of the never-ending paperwork and the formalities while making an investment, then those days are gone. Tech-Start-up lets you invest in a start-up within a minute. Complete all the formalities seamlessly and get onboard with your ideal investment.
